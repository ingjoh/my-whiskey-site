# Proposal

A **Proposal** is a custom sales quote generated by an admin or broker. It serves as a customer-facing container grouping one or more commercial options (**Offers**).

## Details
*   Binds a guest's contact info temporarily without locking the vessel calendar slots.
*   Enables customers to compare multiple distinct alternatives (different vessels, dates, durations, or rates) within a single sales presentation.
*   Contains references to one or more `Offer` documents.
*   Once a single `Offer` is accepted, the parent Proposal transitions to an `accepted` status, and all other companion Offers in the proposal are invalidated.

## Examples

### Good Practice
*   Group a Saturday morning option and a Sunday sunset option as two separate Offers within a single Proposal document.
*   Link a Proposal to a specific Person (the guest recipient) and another Person (the sending agent or staff member).

### Bad Practice
*   Do not store transaction checkout details or calendar reservations directly in the Proposal; delegate these to the `Booking` and `Offer`.
